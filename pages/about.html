Our Story

WE BELIEVE HEALTHY LIVING SHOULD GO WHEREVER YOU GO

Blendify was founded in 2022 by a group of fitness enthusiasts who were tired of skipping their morning smoothies just because they were always on the move. We set out to build the perfect portable blender — powerful enough to handle frozen fruit, compact enough to fit in a gym bag, and beautiful enough to carry with pride.

Today, Blendify serves over 50,000 customers across the country. Every product we design starts with the same question: does this make healthy living easier for real people with real, busy lives?

Our Business Model (B2C) is direct-to-consumer — we design, manufacture, and sell directly to our customers online, cutting out the middleman to keep prices fair and build a direct relationship with every person who blends with us.
