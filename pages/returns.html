ELIGIBILITY

To be eligible for a return, your item must:

  • Be returned within 30 days of the delivery date
  • Be in its original condition (unused or used for testing only)
  • Include all original packaging and accessories
  • Not be damaged due to misuse, drops, or unauthorized modifications

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

  • Items returned after 30 days from delivery
  • Items that have been heavily used or damaged by the customer
  • Promotional or clearance sale items marked as final sale

REFUND METHOD

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. If payment was made by card, the refund will appear within 5–10 business days depending on your bank. Mobile money refunds are typically processed within 24–48 hours.

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS

If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately at support@blendify.store with photos of the damage. We will replace the item or issue a full refund at no additional cost to you.
