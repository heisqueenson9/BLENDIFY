5 Green Smoothies To Kickstart Your Morning

Looking to add more greens to your diet? Our portable blender makes it incredibly easy. Here are five delicious green smoothie recipes that take just three minutes to prepare with your Blendify blender.

1. Classic Spinach & Banana

The perfect beginner smoothie. Packed with potassium and iron, this blend is smooth, creamy, and naturally sweet. Simply add one banana, a handful of fresh spinach, half a cup of Greek yogurt, and one cup of almond milk. Blend until smooth and enjoy!

2. Tropical Green Power

Transport yourself to paradise with this tropical blend. Mix fresh mango, pineapple, spinach, and coconut milk for a refreshing, nutrient-packed boost. The natural sweetness of the fruits masks the earthy taste of greens perfectly.

3. Detox Green Juice

Reset your body with our detox blend. Combine kale, cucumber, green apple, ginger, and lemon juice. This powerful combination supports digestion and provides a natural energy boost to kickstart your day.

4. Creamy Avocado Dream

Indulge guilt-free with this creamy smoothie. Half an avocado, spinach, banana, and oat milk create a silky texture that feels like a treat but packed with healthy fats and fiber.

5. Matcha Green Blend

Combine the power of matcha with fresh greens. One teaspoon of matcha powder, spinach, banana, and almond milk create a smooth, earthy flavor with sustained energy release. Perfect before a workout or morning meeting.

Try these recipes with your Blendify blender and let us know which is your favorite!
