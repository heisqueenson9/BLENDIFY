What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience on Blendify.store.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function. They help us keep your session active and secure your data.

Performance Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site, helping us improve functionality and user experience.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies help us deliver targeted content and track campaign performance across platforms.

Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are sent. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.
