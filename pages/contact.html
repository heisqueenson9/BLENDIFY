FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does the battery last? +
The Blendify PureSip Blender lasts up to 20 blends per full charge. Charging takes approximately 2 hours via USB-C.
Can it blend frozen fruit and ice? +
Yes! All Blendify models can handle soft frozen fruit with ease. For harder ice, we recommend the BlendEase Pro or PowerBlend X which feature a turbo boost motor.
How do I clean my blender? +
Simply add warm water and a drop of dish soap, blend for 20 seconds, then rinse thoroughly. The cup and blade unit are also hand-washable. Do not submerge the motor base.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked money-back guarantee. If you're not happy with your purchase, contact us and we'll arrange a full refund or exchange.
Do you offer free shipping? +
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders. Expedited shipping options are also available at checkout for an additional fee.
Are Blendify products BPA-free? +
Absolutely. All Blendify cups and lids are made with 100% BPA-free, food-grade materials. Your health and safety is our top priority.
