How long does the blender last on a single charge?
Most Blendify models provide 20-30 blends per full charge, depending on the model and usage intensity. The PureSip provides around 20 smooth blends, while our Pro models offer up to 30.
How long does it take to fully charge?
Our portable blenders charge via USB-C in approximately 2 hours from 0% to 100% when using a standard charger.
Can I blend ice and frozen fruit?
Yes! Most Blendify models feature our 6-blade stainless steel system designed specifically to crush ice and frozen ingredients smoothly. For the smoothest results, ensure you have adequate liquid in your blend.
Is it dishwasher safe?
The cup and blades are dishwasher safe (top rack recommended). The base and motor unit should be hand-washed to ensure longevity.
What's your return policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, return it in original condition within 30 days for a full refund, no questions asked.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we ship within Ghana and select African countries. International shipping is coming soon! Check your country at checkout to see if we can deliver to you.
Is the blender loud?
Blendify blenders operate at around 70-75 decibels, similar to a normal conversation. While it's not silent, the sound is minimal for a portable blender and much quieter than traditional kitchen blenders.
Can I use alternative drinks like nut milks?
Absolutely! Our blenders work perfectly with any liquid — whether it's almond milk, coconut milk, oat milk, regular milk, or juice. Mix and match based on your preferences!
